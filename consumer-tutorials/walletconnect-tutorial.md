# How to use WalletConnect on MFEV.cash and add liquidity on MFEVswap

We will learn how to connect MFEV Blockchain cash wallet to various MFEV DApps through WalletConnect feature and add liquidity to MFEVswap through MFEV Blockchain cash wallet.

Mobile OS- Android  
App version used- 0.1.14.144

**Step 1:**

Open your MFEV Blockchain cash wallet on your phone and the interface shown will be similar to the one shown in the image below.

![](../.gitbook/assets/0%20%283%29.jpeg)

**Step 2:**

Navigate to [https://www.MFEVswap.com/](https://www.MFEVswap.com/) on your computer and the interface will be as shown below

![](../.gitbook/assets/1%20%2817%29.png)

Use the “Connect to a wallet” on the right hand top corner and select “WalletConnect”. Now the website will display a QR code.

![](../.gitbook/assets/2%20%2817%29.png)

**Step 3:** On your MFEV cash wallet homepage you can spot a small icon which lets you scan the QR code displayed on [www.MFEVswap.com](http://www.MFEVswap.com/) in the previous step

![](../.gitbook/assets/3%20%283%29.jpeg)

Once the QR code is scanned MFEV cash wallet will ask permission to connect your MFEV cash wallet with MFEVswap dex as shown below. Click on “Connect” to confirm the connection.

![](../.gitbook/assets/4%20%283%29.jpeg)

You have successfully connected your MFEV cash wallet with MFEVswap dex through WalletConnect. You can verify this by double checking the wallet address of your MFEV cash wallet with the wallet address displayed on top hand right corner of MFEVswap dex.

Now we will continue and add liquidity on MFEVswap.

Now click on “Pool” and then on “Add liquidity”. Select MFEV as your first token and fUSD as the second token from the dropdown. Enter the amount of MFEV you want to provide the liquidity for and the corresponding amount of fUSD that should be added will automatically show up according to the price of MFEV.

![](../.gitbook/assets/5%20%2813%29.png)

You can also see your share of the liquidity pool based on the amount of MFEV you want to provide liquidity for.

Click on “Approve fUSD” and confirm the transaction through your wallet.

\(Note: If this is the first time you are providing liquidity on MFEVswap using the above pairs you will have to approve the spend transaction.\)

Once the transaction is confirmed on-chain, click on “Supply” and approve the transaction. You will receive the liquidity pool tokens on your wallet which can be checked under tokens through [www.mediablock.ai](http://www.mediablock.ai/) on your wallet address.

Navigating to **“Pool”** on MFEVswap will also show you list of token pairs with statistics for which you have provided liquidity and other options like add/remove liquidity for that particular token pair as shown below.
